Our Mission

Our mission is to help needy children by providing resources to their schools, offering them an education that can break the cycle of poverty in their lives.

Our Story

We believe that education matters. We believe education is a basic right of every child. We believe education is the surest way to rise out of poverty.

We are currently supporting eight educational programs, five in Kenya and three in India. We are looking for new opportunities to grow the number of schools and children we help in both countries.
